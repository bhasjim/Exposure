Taking great pictures is all about location.

Exposure scours Flickr to help you find the hottest locations to hone your art. Photographers can find locations that other photographers have taken pictures, and use their shots as inspiration. Exposure also allows for people who haven't been to certain locations to get an idea of what the area is like.
